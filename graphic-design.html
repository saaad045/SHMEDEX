Graphic Design FAQs

Answers to common questions about our services

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple projects like business cards or social media graphics can be completed in 2-3 days. More complex projects like full brand identities or packaging designs typically take 1-2 weeks. Our enterprise clients with ongoing design needs benefit from our 24-hour turnaround for most requests.

We deliver all standard file formats based on your project needs. For print, we provide high-resolution PDF, EPS, and sometimes TIFF files. For digital use, we provide PNG, JPG, and often SVG files. All our professional and enterprise packages include source files (AI, PSD) so you have full flexibility for future edits.

While we focus on design, we have established relationships with high-quality printers and can coordinate printing for you if needed. We ensure your designs are print-ready with proper bleeds, color profiles, and specifications. For clients who prefer to handle printing themselves, we provide all the necessary guidelines to ensure your materials print perfectly.
