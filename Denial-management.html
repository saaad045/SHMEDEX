2. Authorization Problems (23%)

3. Coding Errors (19%)

Our Denial Resolution Process

A systematic approach to maximize recoveries

1

Denial Identification & Categorization

We aggregate denials from all sources (ERAs, payer portals, paper EOBs) and categorize by root cause using our proprietary taxonomy.

2

Appeal Preparation

Our certified specialists gather supporting documentation, craft persuasive appeal letters, and ensure compliance with each payer's requirements.

3

Payer Follow-up

We track each appeal through resolution, conducting regular follow-ups with payer representatives to expedite processing.

4

Recovery Posting

All recovered payments are accurately posted to your system with detailed documentation for reconciliation.

5

Prevention Strategy

Monthly reports highlight denial trends and recommend process improvements to prevent future occurrences.
