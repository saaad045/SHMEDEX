Our UI/UX Design Process

1

Discovery & Research

UI/UX Design FAQs

Answers to common questions about our services

UI (User Interface) design focuses on the visual elements - colors, typography, buttons, etc. UX (User Experience) design focuses on the overall feel and functionality of the product. We provide both services to ensure your product is not only beautiful but also intuitive and effective.

We measure success through both qualitative and quantitative metrics including task completion rates, time-on-task, error rates, user satisfaction scores (SUS), conversion rates, and retention metrics. We establish baseline measurements before redesign and track improvements post-implementation.

We frequently work with both. For existing products, we conduct UX audits to identify pain points and opportunities for improvement. For new products, we help shape the experience from the ground up. About 60% of our projects involve redesigning existing interfaces.

Our toolkit includes Figma (primary design tool), Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch, InVision for prototyping, Miro for collaboration, Hotjar for analytics, and various usability testing platforms. We choose tools based on project requirements and are always evaluating new technologies.
