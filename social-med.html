Answers to common questions about our services

You'll typically see initial engagement improvements within 1-2 months, with significant growth in followers and conversions appearing in 3-6 months. Organic social media growth is a marathon, not a sprint. Paid social campaigns can deliver faster results, while organic strategies build lasting value.

The ideal platforms depend on your target audience and business type. B2B companies often see the most success on LinkedIn, while visually-oriented brands thrive on Instagram. Facebook works well for broad reach, TikTok for younger demographics, and Twitter/X for real-time engagement. We'll help you identify the best platforms during our strategy session.

Optimal posting frequency varies by platform:
  • Instagram: 3-5 times per week
  • Facebook: 3-5 times per week
  • LinkedIn: 2-3 times per week
  • Twitter/X: 5-10 times per week
  • TikTok: 3-7 times per week
Quality always trumps quantity. We focus on creating high-value content that resonates with your audience rather than just hitting arbitrary posting quotas.
